Six atributes of a good citizen!

Citizenship is the position or status of being a citizen of a particular country.

The Atributes:

Honesty, Compassion, Respect, Responsibility,Courage, and Toughness

Courage-

Courage is a very important attribute to being a citizen, because it shows how brave you are and that you aren't afraid to try.

Responsibility-

Responsibility is an important atribute on being a citizen. It shows that you are mentally and physically ready to become a citizen and that you can handle it.

Respect-

Respect is one of if not the most important atribute of citizenship. Respect shows how much you care for and how thankful you are able to be in this country.

Honesty-

Honesty is a needed attribute for being a citizen because the people of the place that you are becoming a citizen of have to beileve you and trust you.

Toughness-

Toughness is needed for being a good citizen because you might be bad or fail at doing somethings, but if you're tought you will be able to bounce back and keep going.
